Start-of-session onboarding. Do all of the following:

1. **Read the project structure.** Scan the coworker space directory to understand what exists and what was recently modified.

2. **Read FOCUS.md.** This is the current priorities doc — understand what Stefan is supposed to be working on right now.

3. **Read CHANGELOG.md.** Review every entry to understand what was done in previous sessions, the progression of work, and where things left off.

4. **Check memory.** Review any stored memories for context on Stefan's preferences, project state, and past decisions.

5. **Print a brief status update.** In 5-10 lines, tell Stefan:
   - What the current focus/priority is (from FOCUS.md)
   - What was done last session (from the most recent CHANGELOG entry)
   - What the logical next step is
   - Any open items or decisions that were left unresolved

Don't be generic. Be specific about files, features, and numbers. End with "What do you want to work on?" or similar.
