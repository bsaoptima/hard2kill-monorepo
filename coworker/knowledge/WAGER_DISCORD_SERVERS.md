## HOW THESE SERVERS WORK (Important Context)

### The Middleman System
Most wager servers use a manual escrow system:
1. Two players agree to a wager amount in a public channel
2. Both DM a **middleman** (trusted server member with a special role)
3. Both send money to the middleman (PayPal, CashApp, Venmo, crypto)
4. Players play the match on their console
5. Loser tells middleman, or winner sends screenshot proof
6. Middleman pays the winner

**Pain points Hard2Kill solves:**
- Middlemen aren't always online → matches can't happen
- Middlemen can scam (rare but happens) → trust issues
- Manual payment is slow → PayPal holds, CashApp delays
- Disputes are messy → "he disconnected", "lag", "he cheated"
- No record keeping → middlemen track in spreadsheets or memory

### Server Roles to Look For
- **Admin / Owner** — the person to partner with for tournaments
- **Middleman / MM** — the person to pitch as an affiliate
- **Verified / Trusted** — active players with reputation
- **Looking for Match / LFM** — players actively seeking opponents right now

### Channels to Pay Attention To
- `#looking-for-match` or `#lfm` — where active wagers are posted
- `#wager-results` or `#proof` — where winners post screenshots
- `#middleman-available` — when middlemen are online
- `#general` — where the community chats
- `#rules` — read these before doing anything

### Which Servers to Prioritize
- **Sweet spot: 500-5,000 members** — active enough to have daily wagers, small enough that your DMs and posts get noticed
- Avoid 50k+ member servers at first — you'll get lost. Come back to those once you have social proof.
- Look for servers where `#looking-for-match` has posts from today — that means people are actively wagering, not just lurking
