# Getting the First 50 DAU — The Playbook That Isn't Reddit

> Reddit is a graveyard for self-promotion. You're not doing it wrong — the platform is structurally hostile to founders promoting products. Here's what actually works when you have zero budget and zero existing audience.

---

## THE HONEST TRUTH FIRST

At this stage, there is no scalable channel. There is no hack. Every platform with millions of users (Reddit, TikTok, Twitter) is optimized to filter out self-promotion. The first 50 DAU come from **doing things that don't scale** — manual, one-by-one, uncomfortable outreach. Players Lounge started by literally running FIFA tournaments at bars in New York. That's the energy required.

The good news: you only need 50 people. That's a number you can reach by hand.

---

## CHANNEL 1: INFILTRATE EXISTING WAGER DISCORD SERVERS

This is your highest-ROI channel. There are thousands of Discord servers where people already wager real money on 1v1 matches — using middlemen, honor system, or platforms like CMG. These people are your EXACT customer.

### The Landscape (What Already Exists)

- **Fortnite Wager servers** — some have 90,000+ members (e.g. "Divine Wagers" has 91,580 members). Players bet on 1v1s using middlemen who hold the money.
- **Rocket League Wagers** — players wager credits/items/money on 1v1 and 2v2 matches
- **COD Wagers** — Checkmate Gaming's Discord has ~4,000 members, plus dozens of unofficial servers
- **General "1v1 Wagers" servers** — cross-game communities on DISBOARD, Discord.me, top.gg
- **CS2 Wager servers** — cash 1v1 matches with middlemen

### Why This Matters For You

These communities solve their wager problem with **middlemen** — a human who holds both players' money and pays the winner. This is:
- Slow (need to wait for a middleman to be online)
- Trust-dependent (middlemen can scam)
- Manual (cash app, PayPal, etc.)
- Zero game variety (they play the same 3 AAA titles)

**Hard2Kill automates what middlemen do manually.** That's your pitch. Not "come play our game" — it's "we built a platform that replaces middlemen. Instant escrow, instant payout, no scams."

### Exactly What To Do

**Step 1: Find 20 wager Discord servers (2 hours)**
- Go to [DISBOARD](https://disboard.org/servers/tag/wagers), [Discord.me](https://discord.me/servers/tag/wagers), [top.gg](https://top.gg) and [Discadia](https://discadia.com)
- Search tags: `wagers`, `1v1`, `fortnite-wagers`, `cod-wagers`, `gaming-wagers`, `competitive`
- Join 20 servers. Prioritize ones with 500-5,000 members (big enough to have active players, small enough that you won't get lost)
- Note which ones have active #looking-for-match or #wager-match channels

**Step 2: Be a member first, not a promoter (1 week)**
- Spend the first week just BEING in these servers
- Chat in general channels. React to messages. Ask people about their wager experiences.
- Play the games they play if you can. Be a community member.
- Ask questions like: "Has anyone ever gotten scammed by a middleman?" or "What's the biggest wager you've done?" — these surface pain points that Hard2Kill solves
- DO NOT post any links or mention Hard2Kill during this week

**Step 3: Start conversations, not pitches (Week 2)**
- When someone complains about a middleman being offline, or worries about getting scammed, or says they wish there was a better way: THEN share Hard2Kill
- Frame it as: "I've been working on something that does this automatically — it holds the money in escrow and pays out the winner instantly. Would you try it?"
- DM individuals who seem active and competitive. A personal DM converts 10-50x better than a public post.
- Offer them $10 free on the platform to try it. That's not spam — that's a personal invitation with skin in the game.

**Step 4: Target server admins and middlemen specifically**
- Middlemen are your gateway. They're trusted by the community and they're doing tedious work for free (or small tips).
- DM middlemen: "Hey, I noticed you middleman wagers in this server. I built a platform that automates escrow and payout for 1v1 matches. Would you be interested in being an affiliate? You'd earn a % of every match played by people you refer."
- If a middleman promotes Hard2Kill to their server, that's instant credibility + access to their entire user base.
- Server admins are similar — offer them an affiliate partnership or ask if they'd consider adding Hard2Kill as an "automated wager" option alongside their middleman system.

**Expected result:** 10-30 signups from 20 servers over 2-3 weeks. These are HIGH QUALITY users — they already wager money on games.

## CHANNEL 2: TIKTOK / YOUTUBE SHORTS / REELS (Your Content Engine)

This is a medium-term play. It won't give you 50 DAU in week 1, but it compounds. The key insight from 2025-2026 data:

- **Gaming tutorials and guides** get the highest engagement on TikTok: 6.4% rate
- **Speedruns and challenges** are second: 5.95%
- **Longer videos (60s+)** outperform short clips (6.7% vs 2.95% engagement)
- **Narrative hooks** ("I can't believe this happened") get highest completion rates
- **Post during 5-7 PM**, use trending sounds, respond to every comment

### Exactly What Content To Make

You're not making "gaming content." You're making **"bet on yourself" content.** The hook isn't the game — it's the money on the line.

**Format 1: The Session Journey (Most Viral Format in Gambling Streaming)**
```
Hook: "Starting with $5, let's see how far I get"
Middle: Record 3-5 matches, show the tension, close calls, big moments
End: "I turned $5 into $35 in 20 minutes. Link in bio."
Duration: 60-90 seconds
```

**Format 2: The Challenge**
```
Hook: "I challenged my friend to a $10 match. Loser buys dinner."
Middle: Split screen or alternating POVs of the match
End: Winner celebrates, loser pays up
Duration: 45-60 seconds
```

**Format 3: Tutorial Bait**
```
Hook: "This weapon is broken in GladiatorZ" or "Nobody knows this trick"
Middle: Show the strategy/weapon/tactic in a real match
End: "I used this to win $20. Try it yourself → link in bio"
Duration: 30-45 seconds
```

**Format 4: The Reaction / Close Call**
```
Hook: Clip of the match's most intense moment, freeze frame
Middle: "I had $10 on the line and this happened..."
End: Outcome + your genuine reaction
Duration: 15-30 seconds
```

**Format 5: The Win Streak**
```
Hook: "Win streak day. Let's keep it going."
Middle: Rapid montage of wins, showing the balance going up
End: Final balance reveal
Duration: 30-45 seconds
```

### Posting Strategy

- **3 videos per day** across TikTok + YouTube Shorts + Instagram Reels (same content, reposted)
- **Batch record**: Play for 1-2 hours, record everything, edit into 5-10 clips. That's 2-3 days of content from one session.
- **Use CapCut** for editing (free, fast, has auto-captions)
- **Never say "gambling" or "betting"** on TikTok. Say "playing for money", "cash match", "money match", "put my money where my mouth is"
- **Reply to every comment** in the first hour after posting — this signals engagement to the algorithm, gets viewers to come back (2nd+ view counts)
- **Create a series**: "Day 1 of turning $10 into $1000 on Hard2Kill" — serialized content drives follows because people want to see what happens next

### Timeline

- Week 1-2: Post daily, refine what gets views. Most videos will get 200-500 views. That's fine.
- Week 3-4: Double down on formats that work. 1-2 videos will pop to 5k-50k views.
- Month 2: If you're consistent, 1,000-3,000 followers. Some percentage clicks the link.
- Month 3+: Compounding. Your back catalog keeps getting views.

**Conversion estimate:** ~1-2% of viewers click bio link. ~10% of those sign up. At 10,000 total views/day (achievable by month 2), that's 100 clicks → 10 signups/day.

## CHANNEL 3: YOUR PERSONAL NETWORK (The Fastest Channel)

This feels awkward but it's the fastest path to 10 DAU.

### The Specific Ask

Message 50 people you know personally — friends, coworkers, old classmates, people from communities you're part of. Not a mass blast. Individual messages.

**Template (adjust to your voice):**
```
Hey [name], I've been building this thing — it's a platform where you 
play PvP games against people and bet real money on yourself. Kind of 
like poker but with actual video games.

I need 10 people to test it out this week. Would you be down to play 
a few matches? I'll put $10 in your account so it's totally free to try.

[link]
```

**Why this works:**
- Personal asks convert at 20-40% (vs. <1% for cold outreach)
- You need 50 messages to get 10-20 signups
- Giving them $10 free removes all friction
- Cost: $100-200 total. That's your cheapest possible CAC.

**After they play:**
- Ask them to challenge one friend via challenge link (once you build it)
- If each of your 10 brings 1 friend, you have 20

### The "Pizza Night" Version (What Players Lounge Did)

Players Lounge literally started at bars running FIFA tournaments on slow nights.

**Your version:**
- Invite 5-10 friends over (or set up a Discord voice call)
- Everyone plays Hard2Kill for an hour
- You fund everyone's account ($5-10 each)
- Record the session for TikTok content
- It's a social event, not a product demo
- Do this weekly. "Hard2Kill Fridays."

## CHANNEL 4: KICK STREAMING

Kick is the only major streaming platform that's gambling-friendly (Twitch banned unlicensed gambling streams). It's also much less saturated — fewer streamers means more discoverability.

### Your Own Stream

**What to do:**
- Stream 2-3 times per week on Kick, 1-2 hours per session
- Title: "1v1 MONEY MATCHES — $5 bets — Playing viewers" or "SKILL BETTING — Beat me, take my money"
- Format: play matches on Hard2Kill, narrate, interact with chat
- Invite viewers to challenge you. "Type !play to challenge me for $5"
- This is how poker streamers built their followings — playing live with viewers watching

**Why Kick specifically:**
- Kick's 95/5 revenue split means streamers earn more → more willing to try your platform
- Sponsorship rates on Kick are lower than Twitch → when you're ready for micro-influencers, it's cheaper
- Kick subscriber counts are public → you can easily identify which streamers have engaged audiences
- Gambling/wagering content is allowed (Stake.com's founders own Kick)

### Micro-Influencer Outreach (Week 3+)

**Who to target:**
- Kick streamers with 50-300 average viewers in competitive gaming categories
- Streamers who already do "wager matches" or competitive content
- Avoid huge streamers (expensive, low conversion). Micro-influencers with 200 viewers convert better.

**The pitch (DM on Kick, Twitter, or Discord):**
```
Hey [name], I've been watching your streams — love the competitive 
content. I built a platform called Hard2Kill where players 1v1 for 
real money with automated escrow (no middlemen needed).

Would you be down to try it on stream? I'll preload your account 
with $50 so you can play matches live with your viewers. Plus I'll 
set you up with an affiliate code — you earn 20% of every deposit 
from people who sign up with your code.

No cost to you, keeps your content fresh, and your viewers can 
actually play against you for money.
```

**Cost:** $50 per streamer (preloaded balance) + rev share that only pays when they bring depositing users. Essentially $0 upfront risk beyond the preloaded balance.

**Target:** Contact 20 streamers. 5 will respond. 2-3 will actually stream it. If one has 200 viewers and 5% try it → 10 signups from a single stream.

## CHANNEL 6: PRODUCT HUNT (One-Time Spike)

Worth doing once. Won't sustain DAU but can give you a spike of signups.

**Reality check:** Since September 2024, only 10% of launches get featured on the homepage. But even unfeatured launches get some traffic.

### How to execute:
- Prep for 2 weeks before launch day
- Get 5-10 friends to sign up for Product Hunt accounts (they need to be "real" accounts with some activity — PH's algorithm penalizes suspicious upvote patterns)
- Launch on a **weekend** (less competition for top spots)
- Maker comment: tell the story. "I built a platform where gamers bet money on themselves in PvP matches. Here's why and how."
- Respond to every comment within minutes
- Keep upvotes under 100/hour and geographically diverse (PH flags concentrated upvote spikes)

**Expected result:** 200-2,000 homepage visitors → 20-200 signups → 5-20 who actually play a match. One-time spike, not a sustained channel.

230 million people are active in Facebook gaming groups. These groups are less hostile to promotion than Reddit, especially if you participate first.

### Target Groups:
- Search Facebook for: "competitive gaming", "1v1", "gaming wagers", "esports", "online gaming community"
- Join 10-15 groups with 5,000+ members
- Same playbook as Discord: participate for a week, then share naturally when relevant
- Some groups have "Self-Promotion Saturday" or "Share Your Game" threads — USE THESE

**Key advantage over Reddit:** Facebook groups are run by individual admins, not anonymous mods with power trips. You can DM the admin, explain what you're building, and ask if it's OK to share. Many will say yes, especially if you offer their members a bonus.

## THE NUCLEAR OPTION: PAY PEOPLE TO PLAY

This sounds desperate. It is. It's also exactly what online poker rooms did and it worked.

Early poker rooms hired **prop players** — people paid to sit at tables and play. Their job was to make the room look active. When a real player joined, they'd have someone to play against.

**Your version:**
- Find 5-10 competitive gamers (from wager Discord servers, Fiverr, or gaming forums)
- Pay them $10-15/hour to play on Hard2Kill during peak hours (7-10 PM EST)
- They play real matches against whoever shows up (other prop players, bots, or real users)
- They stream their sessions (bonus content)
- They're active in your Discord talking about matches they played
- Total cost: $100-150/day for 5 players, 2 hours each. That's ~$700/week.

**Is $700/week worth it?** If it means every visitor to Hard2Kill during peak hours can find a match instantly, with real humans, and the platform feels alive — yes. This is what venture-backed companies do with their seed funding. If you don't have the budget, cut to 2-3 prop players at $300-400/week.

**When to stop:** When organic players start filling the peak hour slots themselves (around 30-50 organic DAU).
