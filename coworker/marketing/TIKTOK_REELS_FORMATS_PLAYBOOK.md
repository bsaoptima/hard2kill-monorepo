# TikTok + Instagram Reels Formats Playbook — Geostakes Brazil

> Compiled 2026-05-21 from research on Stake's clipping army, Rainbolt's GeoGuessr library, DamnBruh's TikTok approach, Brazilian betting-creator economy, and 2026 TikTok hook frameworks (34k+ clip dataset).

## Strategic framing

Geostakes is launching unlicensed into Brazil. Paid ads are blocked for unlicensed operators on Meta, TikTok, and Google. Organic content is the only growth channel that scales. This doc is the catalog of formats to spam to make that channel work.

The model is Stake's clipping army adapted to the GeoGuessr-wagering niche, localized to Brazilian Portuguese where it matters.

## Vocabulary discipline (read first)

**Never use in TikTok/Reels captions or text overlays:**
- "bet", "wager", "gambling", "casino", "odds"

**Use instead:**
- "challenge", "play", "compete", "prize", "entry", "match", "stake" (English only — "stake" reads as commerce, not gambling)

In Portuguese:
- ❌ "apostar", "aposta", "cassino"
- ✅ "jogar", "competir", "desafio", "ganhar", "prêmio", "duelo"

Algorithms scan captions and overlay text for the blocked vocabulary and demote. This is the single biggest preventable mistake.

## Critical algorithmic timing (2026 data)

Research analyzing 34,635 clips:
- **1.5 seconds:** algorithm decides if your video advances
- **3 seconds:** completion-rate window — viewer either keeps watching or scrolls
- **2 seconds:** engagement decision

If the hook fails in 1.5 seconds, the video enters a cold start it almost never escapes. Front-load everything. Even tease the resolution in the first 2 seconds.

## Hook frameworks that beat all others

Per the same data analysis:

1. **Cognitive dissonance** — statement the brain can't ignore until it sees the resolution
2. **Contrarian** — "Everyone says X. They're wrong." Top performer at ~2× views of weakest hook
3. **Curiosity** — open loop, tease payoff, delay explanation
4. **Pain-point** — call out the problem the viewer has, immediately show the fix
5. **POV / direct address** — pulls comments, which extend video life

# THE FORMAT CATALOG

30+ specific templates grouped by structural type. Pick 6-8 that fit your bandwidth and run those hard. Don't try all of them.

## A. Founder/operator-protagonist formats

You as the on-camera character. Highest leverage at $0 marginal cost. Ed Craven did this for Stake's first 3 years before they had a single influencer deal.

### 1. "I'll play anyone in Brazil for $X right now"
- **Hook:** "I'll play anyone for $50. Comment your name."
- **Body:** cut to match, real-cash result
- **Tip:** you LOSING produces better content than winning. Lean into it.
- **Variations:** $1 / $5 / $10 / $50 / $100 — each becomes a separate clip

### 2. Build-in-public day counter
- **Hook:** "Day 1 of trying to build a money geography game in Brazil."
- **Body:** screen recording of you fixing a bug, signing up first user, getting first PIX deposit
- **Frequency:** one per day for 30 days = 30 clips

### 3. The honest loss
- **Hook:** "I just lost $200 to a guy in Manaus."
- **Body:** show the match, reveal the loss, acknowledge the skill
- **Why it works:** acknowledged losses build credibility faster than wins. Skeptics convert.

### 4. Stake-ladder journey
- **Hook:** "Started with $1, here's how far I got in 7 matches."
- **Body:** multi-clip series. Each match is one cut.
- **Variations:** $5 ladder, $10 ladder

### 5. PIX notification flex
- **Format:** 30-second tight cut: hit Play → win → PIX hits your phone → repeat
- **Why it works:** Brazilian users have a visceral reaction to the PIX notification sound. Use real audio.

# PRODUCTION SYSTEM

## Bandwidth math for $20K budget

| Tier | Method | Output | Cost |
|---|---|---|---|
| Founder mode | You record matches, edit yourself or via Workana editor | 3-5 clips/day | $0-500/mo |
| Clipping army | Pay Brazilian creators $50-200/M views for clips of your footage | Variable, scales with virality | Pay-on-performance |
| Micro-influencer | Brazilian creators 10k-50k followers, sponsored video | 1-3 sponsored videos | $100-500 each |

**Recommended start:** founder mode + 1 Workana editor at ~$300-500/month. Target 90 clips/month for $300-500. Layer on clippers + influencers only after a clip breaks out (1M+ views).

## Daily routine

1. **Record once per day** — 1-2 hours of bounty matches. Screen + webcam.
2. **Send raw footage to editor** (Workana) with format template noted
3. **Editor cuts into 3-5 clips** following the templates from this doc
4. **Founder approves + posts** across TikTok, Instagram Reels, YouTube Shorts
5. **Reply to every comment in first hour** — engagement velocity boosts distribution

## Cross-platform strategy

Each clip lands on 3 platforms with slight edits to avoid duplicate flags:

| Platform | Adjust |
|---|---|
| TikTok | Raw vertical, native captions, trending sound layered |
| Instagram Reels | +0.5s intro padding, different caption text, different cover frame |
| YouTube Shorts | Add title card, slightly longer (45-60s vs TikTok's 30s) |

**Critical for Instagram:** completion rate is the #1 ranking factor. Front-load the resolution — tease the result in the first 2 seconds.

# WHAT TO SKIP (saves time, avoids penalties)

- ❌ **Multi-account spam / Social Growth Engineers methodology** — TikTok actively detects coordinated inauthentic engagement and penalizes it. "Guaranteed growth" services are mostly scams per multiple 2025 reviews.
- ❌ **Reposting same video to multiple accounts at same times** — instant spam flag
- ❌ **Coordinated engagement** (multiple accounts liking each other's content) — algorithmic penalty
- ❌ **Trending dances / cosplay / generic memes** — wrong audience for skill-money gaming
- ❌ **Engagement pods** — TikTok's 2025 anti-pod algorithm changes broke this entirely

Your moat is **specific, niche, money-forward content**, not volume of generic clips.

# 30-DAY ATTACK PLAN

**Week 1 — establish baseline**
- Set up TikTok + Instagram + YouTube Shorts accounts (Geostakes branded)
- Post 3 clips/day across 6 of the formats above (mix categories A, B, C)
- All in PT-BR. Founder-protagonist heavy.
- Track completion rate and saves per format

**Week 2 — double down on what worked**
- Look at top 3 performing formats by completion rate / saves
- Drop the bottom 3 formats
- Triple production on the winners
- Start mixing in category D (curiosity bait) on the winning structures

**Week 3 — operationalize**
- Hire 1 Brazilian video editor on Workana (~$300-500/mo)
- Hand them this doc + your source footage
- Target 5 clips/day output

**Week 4 — scale via outreach**
- DM 5-10 Brazilian micro-influencers in geography / gaming / side-hustle niches
- Offer $100-200 per sponsored video featuring a real Geostakes match

**Target by day 30:** 90+ clips published, at least one breakout (1M+ views). One breakout often funds the next 90 days of content production.

# RISKS TO WATCH

- **Vocabulary slip:** one "aposta" / "bet" in a caption can demote a whole account. Audit before posting.
- **Affiliate codes / referral codes:** under Brazilian Ordinance 1.231/2024 the operator (you) is liable for what affiliates say. Avoid affiliate marketing entirely in the dirty-launch phase.
- **SPA monitoring:** Brazilian regulator did 51 of 75 early-2025 inspections on influencer relationships with unlicensed operators. Pay influencers as content-production work, not as endorsements. Frame in contracts as creator-economy commissions, not affiliate fees.
- **Account ban velocity:** if TikTok bans an account, don't try to recover — open a new one. The recovery process tells the algorithm "this user is gaming the system."
